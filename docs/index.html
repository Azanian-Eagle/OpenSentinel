The Problem: You Are The Product

Unpaid Labour

Every time you click a traffic light, you are training a corporation's AI model for free. Your time is being monetised without your consent.

Privacy Invasion

Traditional CAPTCHAs often rely on tracking cookies and browser fingerprinting to identify you, following your movements across the entire web.

Accessibility Barriers

Visual puzzles are a nightmare for visually impaired users and often frustrate legitimate users, leading to lower conversion rates.

The Solution: Behavioural Sovereignty

OpenSentinel shifts the paradigm from "Challenge-Response" to "Passive Observation". We verify humanity by analysing how you interact, not what you identify.

1. Passive Telemetry

Our ultra-lightweight sensor (<20kb) observes mouse linearity, speed variance, and keystroke dynamics. Humans are imperfect; bots are precise.

2. Local Analysis

We process data locally where possible. We do not need your identity, only the "shape" of your interaction. This ensures POPIA and GDPR compliance.

3. Rust Performance

The backend is built in Rust (Actix-web), offering blazing fast verification speeds and memory safety that legacy systems cannot match.

About Azanian Eagle

Azanian Eagle is a South African open-source initiative dedicated to reclaiming digital independence. We believe that security tools should be accessible, transparent, and respectful of human rights.

In an era where personal data is the new oil, we are building the infrastructure for a privacy-first internet. Our software is forged in Mzansi, but built for the world. We adhere to the highest standards of code quality and ethical engineering.

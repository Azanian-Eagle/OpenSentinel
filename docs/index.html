About Azanian Eagle

Azanian Eagle is a proudly South African open-source organisation committed to digital sovereignty and privacy. Forged in the heart of Mzansi, we build robust tools that empower developers to secure their applications without relying on data-harvesting Big Tech monopolies.

Our mission is to provide local-first, privacy-respecting alternatives that honour the user's dignity. We believe that security should not come at the cost of privacy.
